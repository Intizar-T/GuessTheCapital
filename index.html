    The aim of this website is to help to learn the capitals of worldwide countries in a fun way.

    The rules of the game are simple:

    1. Search for the capital of a given country in the searchbox
    2. Increase your net score (recorded at the bottom) of the webpage by guessing the capitals right
    3. Compare your previous game's result with the current one to track your performance

    Notes: (1) The capital of a provided country will be demonstrated on the map after your guess. (2) You can visit the previously guessed countries/capitals on the map by hovering on the history. (3) In order to restart the game, reload the page.
